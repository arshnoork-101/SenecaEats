About Seneca Eats

Welcome to Seneca Eats, your premier dining destination across Seneca Polytechnic's campuses. We are committed to providing high-quality, diverse, and nutritious food options to our students, faculty, and staff.

Our mission is to create a welcoming and inclusive dining environment that caters to various dietary preferences and requirements. We take pride in offering a wide range of culinary choices, from international cuisines to local favorites, all prepared with fresh ingredients and sustainable practices.

We are constantly evolving and improving our services based on community feedback. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we welcome your suggestions to help us better serve you.

Seneca Eats Services Overview

This overview outlines the various food-related services and support available to Seneca College students.

I. Food Support Programs
SSF Eats – Food Support Program (Food Bank)
Provides monthly food assistance to full-time Seneca students in need, offered as food hampers or grocery gift cards. This program is designed to support students facing food insecurity and ensure they can focus on their academic success.
Eligibility: Must be enrolled full-time during the Summer 2025 semester.
Registration: Monthly registration is required and limited to 400 students.
Apply Now: SSF Eats Food Support Program
II. Campus Facilities
Microwave Stations
Convenient microwave stations available across campus for students to heat their meals.
Vending Machines
24/7 access to snacks and beverages through strategically placed vending machines.
Convenience Store
On-campus convenience store offering a variety of snacks, beverages, and essential items.
III. Special Services
Cultural Events
Special dining events celebrating diverse cultures and cuisines throughout the academic year.
Exam Relief Service
Special meal services and extended hours during exam periods to support students.
